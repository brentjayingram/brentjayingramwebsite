About Me

I’m a Cloud & Automation Engineer. I design, harden, and automate infrastructure in AWS and Azure for workloads that companies actually run revenue on.

Day to day, that means Terraform, resilience patterns, incident recovery, and making sure backups actually restore. I like infrastructure that can take a punch.

I’ve led multi-cloud rebuilds after security incidents, built cross-account backup vaults that can’t be mass-deleted, lead multiple projects on time and under budget, migrated legacy workloads, and stood up dashboards that give leadership the truth instead of “everything's fine 🐶☕🔥”.

I’m based in the Atlanta area, but originally from California.

Outside work: I'm a marathon runner, mentor high school students, wood worker and all around tinkerer.
